How It Works

Three simple steps to visualize any product in augmented reality.

1
👆

Pick a Product

Browse our catalog and select the item you want to visualize in your space.

2
📷

Open Camera

Hit "View in AR" to launch the camera experience directly in your browser.

3
🎯

Place & Explore

Point at a surface, tap to place, then scale and rotate to see it perfectly.

4
📸

Capture & Share

Screenshot your AR placement and share it with friends or your design team.

⚡ Capabilities

Powered by WebXR

Enterprise-grade AR technology running directly in your browser — no downloads, no plugins.

📐

Surface Detection

Automatically detects floors, tables, and walls for accurate product placement.

🔄

Scale & Rotate

Adjust size and orientation of any placed product with intuitive controls.

👤

Face Try-On

TensorFlow.js face tracking places sunglasses and watches exactly on your face.

🎨

Paint Preview

See any paint color on your walls instantly with adjustable opacity blending.

📸

AR Screenshots

Capture high-quality photos of your AR placement to share or save.
